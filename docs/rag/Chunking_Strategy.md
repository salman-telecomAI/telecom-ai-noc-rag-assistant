# Chunking Strategy

## Objective

Prepare enterprise telecom documents for Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG).

## Process

Raw Documents
↓
Cleaning
↓
Chunking
↓
Embeddings
↓
Vector Database

## Recommended Settings

- Chunk Size: 500–1000 characters
- Chunk Overlap: 100–200 characters
- Preserve document structure
- Keep headings with content

## Benefits

- Better semantic search
- Improved retrieval accuracy
- Reduced hallucinations
- Faster query performance

## Expected Outcome

Documents are divided into meaningful chunks suitable for embedding generation and semantic search.
