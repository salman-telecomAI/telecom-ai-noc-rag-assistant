# Document Chunking

## Objective

Split telecom documents into smaller chunks before embedding generation.

## Workflow

Document

↓

Chunking

↓

Embeddings

↓

ChromaDB

↓

Semantic Search

## Benefits

- Better retrieval
- Higher accuracy
- Faster search
- Reduced hallucinations

## Expected Outcome

Every telecom document is divided into overlapping chunks before vectorization.
